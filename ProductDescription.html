Product Description

This app will allow students to personalize their character for fun and set their daily study plans where students don't have to miss studying and you can take valid breaks in between that helps you process the information better. Have a variety of study methods such as the Feymann Technique, Traffic Light Method, Blurt Method, Flash Cards, and much more. Students can choose to study in any ambience by themselves or with a friend and personalize their own sound track. Additionally, they can earn study points while they study and use those study points to unlock more study documents according to the subject they are studying in, and use those study points to unlock more sound track as well.
