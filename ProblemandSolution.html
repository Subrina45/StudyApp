Problem and Solution

Problem

This app solves the problem of students who tend to overstudy and have a hard time taking breaks or students who don't have an effective study method and have a hard time studying at a consistent manner.

Solution

This app does present a solution for students because it has a desired time plan set for the day or the night and it will automatically sort how many hours you should be studying with your breaks along with the study methods and sound track you prefer.
